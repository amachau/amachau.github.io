THE FOUNDER FOUNDED THIS MEDIA PLATFORM Hi! I'm Lee, Founder and Editor-in-Chief. We’ve all learnt that “giant trees grow from small seeds”, so I have always felt the need to help those who had the drive to succeed. Even as I reflect on the days when I overheard my peers complain about the lack of support they received when starting their businesses or witnessing young adults in inner city neighborhoods steer into the wrong direction of life, I knew I wanted to help millennials achieve their goals. Leading with Lee Magazine was created to give aspiring and seasoned young adults a platform to learn, grow, and have a voice. ​ To date, we have provided a media platform for brands and startups that has successfully gained exposure as well as connections for their businesses and personal brands. ​ More about Lee
Leading with Lee Magazine Leading with Lee Magazine is a black owned media outlet and an award winning digital resource guide for emerging and seasoned entrepreneurs, trendsetters, and game changers. Its where Boss Millennials share brand stories, network, build, and gain knowledge on how to start and grow their own businesses or personal brands. Our readers have 24 hour access to a plethora of business and branding tips ranging from our exclusive guest interviews to our e-Guides. ​ In addition, we cover pop culture, fashion, entertainment, and community News.
